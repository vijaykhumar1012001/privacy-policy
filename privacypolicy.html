Meal ResQ Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Meal ResQ. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our food donation app.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you register, we collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and any other information you provide.
  • Donation Details: Information related to the food donations you make, including details about the type and quantity of food.
  • Delivery Agent Information: Details about delivery agents, including their names, contact information, and location data, used for coordinating deliveries.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, including your IP address, browser type, and operating system.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide and Manage Our Services: To process donations, manage delivery logistics, and improve the app's functionality.
  • To Communicate with You: To send notifications, updates, and respond to your inquiries.
  • To Analyze and Improve Our Services: To understand user behavior and enhance the user experience.
  • To Comply with Legal Obligations: To meet legal requirements and enforce our terms of service.

4. Sharing Your Information

  • With Delivery Agents: We share necessary information with delivery agents to facilitate the pickup and delivery of food donations.
  • With Third-Party Service Providers: We may share information with vendors who perform services on our behalf, such as hosting services, analytics, and payment processing.
  • As Required by Law: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to legal requests.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to safeguard your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

  • Access and Update: You have the right to access and update your personal information.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of receiving promotional communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in those communications.
  • Deletion: You may request the deletion of your personal information, subject to certain legal restrictions.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our app. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
